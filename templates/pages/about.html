Welcome to Car Zone

We are a multi-channel platform with coverage and presence across vehicle types and value-added services. Our platforms operate under several brands. Through these platforms, we enable new and used automobile customers, vehicle dealerships, vehicle OEMs and other businesses to buy and sell their vehicles in a simple and efficient manner. Our vision is to create an automotive digital ecosystem which connects automobile customer and other stakeholders.

Car Zone is a next generation ecommerce platform for pre-owned cars. We provide the best experience for car buyers by offering a wide assortment of certified cars that are home delivered in a click of a button while sellers get the best price of their vehicles in less than 1 hour.
